# Accounts Payable Audit Checklist

## Review Areas
- Supplier invoice validation
- PO and GRN matching
- Payment approval evidence
- Supplier master data controls
- AP aging review
- Duplicate payment prevention
- Cut-off testing

## Evidence Required
AP aging, invoice samples, purchase orders, GRNs, three-way match reports, payment approval logs, supplier master changes, and exception reports.

## Common Gaps
Missing GRN support, manual approvals outside DOA, duplicate suppliers, aged unpaid invoices, and weak invoice exception tracking.
