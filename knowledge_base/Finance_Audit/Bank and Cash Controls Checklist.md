# Bank and Cash Controls Checklist

## Review Areas
- Monthly bank reconciliations
- Review sign-offs
- Aged reconciling items
- Bank signatory controls
- Cash counts
- Segregation of duties

## Evidence Required
Bank reconciliations, bank statements, reconciliation review sign-offs, cash count sheets, bank mandate or signatory list, and unresolved item trackers.

## Common Gaps
Late reconciliations, unresolved reconciling items, unsupported cash balances, and outdated signatory records.
