# Finance Audit Procedure

## Purpose
This procedure defines the SCP finance audit approach for reviewing governance, financial controls, transaction evidence, reconciliations, tax compliance, reporting, and remediation monitoring.

## Core Steps
1. Confirm finance policies, accounting responsibilities, and approval authority.
2. Review DOA compliance for sampled transactions.
3. Test accounts payable controls using invoice, PO, GRN, and payment approval samples.
4. Test accounts receivable controls using AR aging, customer statements, and collection evidence.
5. Review bank reconciliations, cash controls, and unresolved reconciling items.
6. Review fixed asset register completeness, capitalization, disposals, and physical verification.
7. Review VAT returns, reconciliations, tax invoices, filing, and payment evidence.
8. Review trial balance, financial statements, close checklist, and balance sheet reconciliations.
9. Review internal audit findings, finance risk register, and remediation tracker.

## Expected Evidence
Finance policy, DOA matrix, trial balance, financial statements, bank reconciliations, AP aging, AR aging, VAT returns, invoice samples, payment approvals, fixed asset register, and internal audit reports.
