# Accounts Receivable Audit Checklist

## Review Areas
- AR aging review
- Customer statement reconciliation
- Collection follow-up
- Credit note approval
- Bad debt provisioning
- Revenue cut-off

## Evidence Required
AR aging, customer statements, collection logs, credit note approvals, bad debt review, sales invoices, and revenue reconciliation evidence.

## Common Gaps
Aged receivables without escalation, unsupported credit notes, incomplete customer reconciliations, and delayed bad debt assessment.
