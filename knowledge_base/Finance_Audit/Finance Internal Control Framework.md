# Finance Internal Control Framework

## Control Domains
- Governance and policies
- Delegation of authority
- Budgeting and budgetary control
- Accounts payable
- Accounts receivable
- Bank and cash
- Fixed assets
- VAT and tax
- Financial reporting and reconciliations
- Internal audit, risk, and compliance monitoring

## Minimum Control Expectations
Finance controls should include documented policies, segregation of duties, approval thresholds, periodic reconciliations, exception reporting, evidence retention, and management review.

## Evidence Standards
Controls should be supported by approved policies, transaction samples, reconciliations, aging reports, financial reports, audit logs, review sign-offs, and action trackers.
