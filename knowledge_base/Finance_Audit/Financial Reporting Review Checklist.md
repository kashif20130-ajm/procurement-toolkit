# Financial Reporting Review Checklist

## Review Areas
- Trial balance review
- Financial statement preparation
- Month-end close checklist
- Balance sheet reconciliations
- Management review sign-offs
- Reporting adjustments

## Evidence Required
Trial balance, financial statements, close checklist, balance sheet reconciliations, journal approval evidence, and management review sign-offs.

## Common Gaps
Unreconciled balance sheet accounts, late close activities, unsupported journals, missing review sign-offs, and inconsistent reporting packs.
