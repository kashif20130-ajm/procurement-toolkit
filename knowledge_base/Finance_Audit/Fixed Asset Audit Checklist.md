# Fixed Asset Audit Checklist

## Review Areas
- Fixed asset register completeness
- Capitalization policy compliance
- Asset tagging
- Physical verification
- Depreciation review
- Disposal approvals

## Evidence Required
Fixed asset register, capitalization approvals, asset tags, physical verification results, depreciation schedules, and disposal approval records.

## Common Gaps
Incomplete register fields, untagged assets, missing verification evidence, unsupported disposals, and outdated depreciation assumptions.
