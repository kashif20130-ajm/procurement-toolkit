# VAT Compliance Audit Checklist

## Review Areas
- VAT return preparation
- Output and input tax reconciliation
- Tax invoice compliance
- Filing and payment evidence
- VAT adjustments and exceptions
- Retention of tax records

## Evidence Required
VAT returns, tax workings, VAT reconciliations, tax invoices, filing confirmations, payment evidence, and exception logs.

## Common Gaps
Unreconciled VAT balances, missing tax invoices, late filings, unsupported adjustments, and incomplete review evidence.
