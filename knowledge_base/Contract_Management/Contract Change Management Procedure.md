# Contract Change Management Procedure

## Purpose
Define controls for contract variations, addenda, PO amendments, subcontracting, delegation, assignment, term extensions, and other post-signature changes. The procedure ensures material changes receive proper approval and are traceable to the original contract.

## Scope
This procedure applies to all signed contracts and pending contracts where scope, value, term, liability, data processing, subcontracting, assignment, payment, service levels, or termination rights are changed. It includes Dubai Law No. 5 of 2026 Etimad/client-entity approval checks where applicable.

## Key Principles
- Contract changes must be documented before implementation.
- A PO amendment is not a substitute for a written addendum where contractual rights or obligations change.
- Any cumulative contract variation exceeding 10% of the original contract value must trigger formal DoA re-approval and a written Addendum.
- For contracts falling under Dubai Law No. 5 of 2026, any variation or delegation of work to a subcontractor must be verified against the Etimad platform and approved by the client entity to avoid systemic breach.
- Change records must update the register, risk rating, obligations, and audit trail.

## Procedure Or Framework
1. Log the proposed change with contract ID, change type, business reason, original contract value, cumulative variation value, owner, and required date.
2. Assess whether the change affects scope, value, term, liability, data, subcontracting, assignment, service levels, payment, or termination.
3. Calculate cumulative variation percentage against original contract value.
4. If cumulative variation exceeds 10%, obtain formal DoA re-approval and prepare a written Addendum.
5. If subcontracting, delegation, or assignment is involved, check whether prior notice, consent, Etimad verification, or client entity approval is required.
6. Attach approvals, addendum, updated PO, Etimad/client approval evidence where applicable, and legal review notes to the contract file.
7. Execute the addendum or approved change document.
8. Update the contract register, obligation tracker, renewal calendar, risk matrix, and audit checklist.

## Roles And Responsibilities
| Role | Responsibilities |
|---|---|
| Business owner | Initiates change request and confirms business need. |
| Contract manager | Logs change, calculates variation percentage, tracks approvals and evidence. |
| Legal counsel | Determines addendum need and reviews contractual impact. |
| Finance / procurement | Confirms budget, PO impact, and DoA re-approval. |
| Client entity approver | Approves covered Dubai Law No. 5 of 2026 variations/delegations through required process. |
| Authorized signatory | Signs addendum after approvals are complete. |

## Evidence Required
- Change request record.
- Original contract value.
- Cumulative variation calculation.
- DoA approval where threshold applies.
- Written addendum where required.
- PO amendment if applicable.
- Etimad verification and client entity approval where applicable.
- Updated register and obligations.

## Assessment Checklist
- Is the change logged before implementation?
- Is cumulative variation calculated correctly?
- Does the change exceed 10% of original contract value?
- Is DoA re-approval documented?
- Is a written Addendum prepared where required?
- Does subcontracting/delegation require notice, consent, or Etimad/client approval?
- Are register, obligations, and audit trail updated?

## Common Gaps
- Informal scope changes implemented through email.
- PO amendment used despite contract-scope change.
- Variation percentage calculated only for the latest change, not cumulative changes.
- Subcontracting approved operationally without contract review.
- Etimad/client approval evidence missing for covered contracts.
- Signed addendum not linked to the original contract record.

## Recommended Improvements
- Add a change request form to the intake workflow.
- Automate variation percentage calculation in the tracker.
- Require legal review for all subcontracting, delegation, and assignment changes.
- Add mandatory DoA and addendum fields for variations above 10%.
- Audit change-control records monthly.
