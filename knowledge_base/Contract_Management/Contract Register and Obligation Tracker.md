# Contract Register And Obligation Tracker

## Purpose
Define the records and tracking requirements for active contracts, renewal dates, signed copies, obligations, owners, and evidence. The register and obligation tracker provide the operational backbone for contract governance.

## Scope
This document applies to every contract that is drafted, reviewed, signed, amended, renewed, expired, or terminated. It includes signed copies, renewal notices, obligations, audit evidence, and change-control references.

## Key Principles
- The register is the single source of truth.
- Every active contract must have an owner, value, term, risk level, and signed-copy link.
- Obligations must be converted from clause language into operational tasks.
- Renewal notice dates must be tracked separately from end dates.
- Change-control records must link to the original contract ID.

## Procedure Or Framework
Minimum register fields:
- Contract ID.
- Counterparty.
- Contract type.
- Business owner.
- Status.
- Risk level.
- Total contract value.
- Effective date.
- End date.
- Renewal notice date.
- Auto-renewal flag.
- Data involved flag.
- Signed copy link.
- Key notes.

Minimum obligation fields:
- Obligation ID.
- Contract ID.
- Clause reference.
- Obligation summary.
- Owner.
- Frequency.
- Due date.
- Status.
- Evidence or notes.

## Roles And Responsibilities
| Role | Responsibilities |
|---|---|
| Contract manager | Maintains register fields, signed-copy links, renewal alerts, and data quality. |
| Business owner | Owns operational obligations and confirms renewal decisions. |
| Legal counsel | Identifies legal obligations and high-risk terms for tracking. |
| Finance / procurement | Confirms value, PO, payment, and renewal economics. |
| Privacy / security | Confirms data-bearing obligations and evidence. |

## Evidence Required
- Signed contract file.
- Register entry.
- Obligation tracker entry.
- Renewal decision record.
- Approval trail.
- Change/addendum references.
- Evidence of completed obligations.

## Assessment Checklist
- Does every active contract have mandatory fields complete?
- Is the signed copy linked?
- Are notice dates calculated and visible?
- Are obligations assigned to owners?
- Are overdue obligations escalated?
- Are addenda and variations linked?
- Is retention/archive information complete?

## Common Gaps
- Contract saved but not entered in the register.
- Renewal end date captured but notice date missing.
- Obligations assigned to departments rather than named owners.
- Evidence of completed obligations not retained.
- Addenda stored separately from parent contract.

## Recommended Improvements
- Freeze mandatory register columns and protect formulas where possible.
- Use dropdown lists for status, risk, contract type, and obligation status.
- Review renewal windows monthly.
- Audit signed-copy links and mandatory fields.
- Add alerts for overdue obligations and missing owners.
